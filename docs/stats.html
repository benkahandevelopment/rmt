Stats

The "Stats" tab allows you to input figures for various matchday statistics.

Kwick Maffs

All stats are rendered as bar charts out of 16. The tool will make the bars as long as the percentage of totals.

For example, if the Shots On Target are 3 (home) and 9 (away), the home team's bar will be 4 in length (3 / ( 3 + 9 ) x 16 = 4), while the away team will be 12 in length (either 16 - 4 = 12 or 9 / ( 3 + 9 ) x 16 = 12)
