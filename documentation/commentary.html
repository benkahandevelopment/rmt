Commentary

The "Commentary" tab allows you to add, remove and edit match commentary updates.

To add a new commentary update, simply fill in the fields at the top and press the "Add Commentary Update" button.

Icon

Commentary
This field is your match update text. This field accepts reddit markup

Editing/Removing Entries

To edit an entry, hover over the entry until the edit button appears on the right hand side .

Once clicked, the "Edit Commentary Entry" modal will popup, allowing you to edit the same fields as when submitting it initially.

To remove the entry entirely, click "Delete Entry". A prompt will appear for confirmation.
